The Project

Creative Heritage Work

The ASA NTI Card Game is a creative work inspired by elements of the Asante Kingdom. It draws from selected systems, stories, symbols, and personalities associated with the Kingdom to shape its gameplay and visual identity.

It is an artistic interpretation — not a scholarly reconstruction — and is offered with the deepest respect for the living culture from which it draws inspiration.

"I wanted to create something that a child in Accra could pick up and immediately recognise something of themselves in — and that the Ghanaian diaspora could use to feel connected across distance."

Every element of the game — from the card designs to the rulebook language — has been shaped by deep engagement with Asante history, symbolism, and storytelling tradition. ASA NTI is a love letter to Ghanaian cultural identity.

Partners & Support

Who Stands with Us

Mensah-Bonsu Foundation

ASA NTI has been funded by the Mensah-Bonsu Foundation Creative Grant initiatives, which are committed to celebrating African heritage through contemporary media. Their backing has made it possible to invest in the quality of production and design that this story deserves.

Newcomma Initiative

Through the Newcomma Initiative, ASA NTI is part of a broader effort to develop and distribute Africa-inspired games and creative works that connect communities, spark cultural pride, and introduce the richness of African traditions to new audiences worldwide.

Our Vision

Heritage is not a museum piece.

It is a living, breathing set of values, stories, and aesthetics that belong at the table — at every game night, every family gathering, every community event. ASA NTI exists to make that possible.
